Oswald the Dog

Hi, I'm Ozzy the Miniature Schnauzer.

I'm 8 human years old and reside in East York. When I'm not sniffing the world around me I enjoy challenging big dogs to duels and barking at cyclists, joggers, children and anyone who dares to walk past my door.

I like travelling and have been on a number of vacations. My favourite destination was BC because there was so much nature to sniff and pee on. I wasn't too fond of Las Vegas because the asphalt was too hot on my paws.

My favourite thing in the world is food. I don't discriminate, I'll eat it all. My biggest pet peeve is Golden Retrievers. What is UP with those guys? Their scent makes me wanna fight them.
